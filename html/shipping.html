Shipping Information

We strive to provide fast and reliable shipping for all our customers. Below are the details of our shipping policy.

Delivery Time

Orders are processed within 2-3 business days. Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days, while expedited shipping takes 2-3 business days.

Shipping Charges

Shipping charges vary based on location and order value. Free shipping is available for orders above $50.

International Shipping

We ship internationally. Delivery times and charges vary by destination. Customers are responsible for any customs duties.

Tracking Orders

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email.

Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, you can return it under the following conditions.

Return Window

Returns are accepted within 90 days of purchase, provided the product is unused and in its original packaging.

Refund Process

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after the returned item is received and inspected.

Return Shipping

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is defective or incorrect.

Exchange Policy

Exchanges are available for defective or damaged items. Contact our support team for assistance.
